# 👋 Hey, this is Monocle Exchange 

Yes, we are building an open source Monero Exchange based on Haveno Network. In fact, we just started, **✨July 1st, 2026✨**. That's when we made our first commit. Join us on our journey 🚀

## 🍿 An exchange built for the Monero and open source community
Monocle Exchange 💗 loves helping people buy and sell Monero.

## ❤️ Open Source
We want open source projects to succeed, we are an open source project ourselves. That's why giving back to the open source community is at the core of everything we do.

## 🔐 Security
We want to keep our users as safe as possible. If you notice a vulnerability, please follow our project's `SECURITY.md`

## 💬 Feedback
We’re good listeners. Our [Meta repository](https://github.com/monocle-exchange/meta) is designed to gather feedback

## 💚 Community
We want to build a community of people interested in privacy, Monero, and open source projects. Get involved in our [discussions](https://github.com/orgs/monocle-exchange/discussions).

## 🤝 Join us
We cannot do it alone. We welcome contributions! Check out individual repositories for contributing guides, open issues, and ways to get involved. Look for `good first issue` or `help-wanted` labels to get started. Once we are established we will have the following [roles](https://github.com/monocle-exchange/roles) available.
